PROFESSIONAL

Skills in Research, Design & Strategy

Requirements Gathering

Requirements gathering and design based on participatory workshops, focus groups, semi-structured interviews, user studies, and card sorting. Close collaboration with customer success team to understand customer needs. Conducting customer feedback sessions. Ethnographic methods such as participant observation and analysis of artifacts. Micro-analysis of video records to understand user interaction.

Analysis & Evaluation

Usability testing methods including simulation in high-fidelity environments, heuristic evaluation, scenario-based testing, cognitive walkthrough, think aloud protocol, and video review. Tagging, tracking, and analyzing product feature engagement. Controlled experiments and survey design. Conducting remote user testing using video conferencing, screen recording, and screen control. Conducting usability testing for new features and validating acceptance criteria on development environments before releasing.

Product Analytics: Pendo.io, Google Analytics
Conferencing tools: Zoom
Statistical packages: SPSS

Design & Development

Rapid prototyping using wireframing, HTML/CSS, and photo manipulation techniques. Creating high fidelity designs in Sketch and prototyping using InVision or HTML/CSS. Building design components in Sketch. Creating and managing design systems with Sketch + InVision (Craft) DSM. Learning how to translate design systems into React Storybook. Collaborating directly with developers to determine technical feasibility, executing designs, and testing feature work.

Design tools: Sketch, InVision, Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Lightroom, Acrobat Pro)
Development Tools: Git, Sublime Text
Languages: HTML, CSS, some Javascript
Content Management Systems: WordPress

Design Strategy & Agile Processes

Collecting and analyzing feedback from multiple sources to strategize design solutions to address customer needs. Collaborating closely with Product Managers and Engineering Managers to brainstorm, prioritize, and execute new product features.

Implementing an integrated research & design process that works with agile development cycles. Co-leading a cross-functional team, sprint planning, ticket refinement, and mid-sprint standups. Preparing internal communications to customer success about new feature releases and scheduling. Learning how to prepare external communications to customers within the product using Pendo.io.

Agile tools: Jira
